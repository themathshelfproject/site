How does it work?

The rules

Published

03 October 2021

As mentioned here, The Math Shelf Project is about “reading” math books by solving all the exercises. To keep myself accountable, here are some ground rules I’ll be following.

  1. All solutions will be graded by someone with graduate training in mathematics
  2. The above grader will only mark solutions as right or wrong
    • Therefore, if I get a problem wrong, I will have to “debug” and fix it myself.
  3. All solutions will be posted here as a learning resource (and for anyone to critique).
